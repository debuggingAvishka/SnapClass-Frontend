The Teacher's Journey

STEP 01

Secure Login

Sign in securely using your teacher credentials. All classroom data, attendance records, and subject information remain protected and synced.

STEP 02

Create Subjects

Set up new courses in seconds. Generate unique subject codes and organize your classroom without manual paperwork.

STEP 03

Student Enrollment

Share the generated course code or QR code with students. Enrollment happens instantly and class rosters update automatically.

STEP 04

FaceID Attendance

Upload classroom photos and let AI identify registered students. Attendance is generated automatically within seconds using facial recognition.

STEP 05

Voice ID Attendance

Students speak a short phrase and SnapClass verifies their identity using voice biometrics, enabling a futuristic AI-powered roll call.

STEP 06

Attendance Reports

Review, verify, and save attendance records instantly. Generate accurate reports and maintain a complete history for every subject.
